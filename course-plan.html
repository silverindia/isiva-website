1. Institute & Department

Selecting an institute and department auto-fills Vision/Mission, PEOs, POs and PSOs below wherever that data has been configured. You can still edit any auto-filled text. Choose "Other College / Institution" if you're joining from outside Mahendra Educational Institutions — every field there is yours to fill in.

PDF Header & Branding

This controls the letterhead at the top of the downloaded PDF only — it doesn't change anything on this page. Both header lines are pre-filled from your institute/department selection above but are fully editable. The left-hand logo is your college's main crest; the right-hand logo is optional (e.g. a trust emblem, NAAC/NBA seal, or affiliating-university mark).
